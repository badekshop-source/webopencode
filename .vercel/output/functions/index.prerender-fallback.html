How It Works

Get your Bali SIM in 5 simple steps. No complicated procedures, no hidden fees.

01

Choose Your Plan

Browse our eSIM and SIM card options. Select the duration that fits your Bali stay - from 3 to 30 days.

02

Secure Payment

Complete checkout with Midtrans. We accept all major credit cards (VISA, Mastercard, JCB, AMEX, UnionPay).

03

Upload Documents

After payment, upload your passport photo and IMEI number. Our AI system approves clear documents instantly.

04

Get Your QR Code

Once approved, receive your QR code via email. Show this at our Ngurah Rai Airport counter for SIM pickup.

Stay Connected

Enjoy unlimited 4G/5G data throughout Bali. Our support team is available 24/7 if you need any assistance.
